How To Use Codex

Get started by creating a notebook by pressing the + icon by "NOTEBOOKS" or pressing Ctrl+N or Cmd+N




From there you can start writing your notes.

Codex automatically saves pages when you switch to another page, and when you exit the program. You can manually save by pressing Ctrl-S or Cmd-S.

Codex also automatically checks for updates when you open the program.



You can get a more minimalized look while taking notes by hiding the sidebar (View > Toggle Sidebar, Ctrl-D or Cmd-D) and zooming in (View > Zoom In)
